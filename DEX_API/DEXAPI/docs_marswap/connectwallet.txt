How to connect to MARSWAP Exchange

After you have successfully added the chosen network to your wallet, and bridged native COIN, the next step is to connect your wallet to Marswap Exchange.

Go to: https://zk.marswap.exchange

    Click on the "connect wallet" button.

    Choose your wallet provider.

    Make sure you're connected to the network you intend to use.

    Press "connect" to authenticate.

That's it! You're connected.
